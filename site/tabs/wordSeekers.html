"Word Seekers" is the game for upgrading your attentiveness. Within a field of many letters you have to find words (which are written below the field). Every game has a unique words for you to find. Words may be written horizontally (from left to right) or vertically (from top to bottom).

Who wins?

Who found most words!
If both players found equal amount of words then everybody is a winner. However, since "Way" and "Spiral of Victories" contain only defeated guys no changes are made to both.
Note that game may be finished before game time runs up if all words are found.

What powerups may be used?

First Letter
Show you first letter of random word from the list. It saves time for finding a word.
-20 Letters
Removes 20 random letters, which are not included in any word. Generally talking, it removes rubbish.
